CementJS is a Masonry alternative.
Basic usage:

$('.container').cement({
  columns: 5
});
Options availables:

  • - columns
  • - columnMinWidth
  • - brickSelector
  • - horizontalGutter
  • - verticalGutter
  • - transitionDuration
